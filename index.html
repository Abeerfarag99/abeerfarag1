Meals 4 less

Type

Web Design

Date

June 2024

Role

Product designer

Client

Meals 4 less

The main goal of the Meals 4 Less website is to promote the Meals 4 Less app, encouraging users to download it and start reducing food waste in their daily lives, inform them about the impact of food waste and how the Meals 4 Less app helps them make budget-friendly, sustainable choices and Highlight the app's benefits and make the download process seamless with clear calls-to-action.

The challenge

Raising Awareness and Driving Engagement for Food Waste Reduction The main challenge was to attract and engage users who may not be fully aware of the impact of food waste or how the Meals 4 Less app could help. Many potential users might not realize that small changes in meal planning and ingredient use can lead to significant waste reduction and savings. Additionally, promoting a new app requires a strong first impression to encourage users to download and engage with it.

My solution

Highlighting Real Benefits by using clear and concise content, the website outlines how the app helps users reduce waste and save money, making it appealing to a wide audience. Showcasing app features through interactive elements allows users to see how easy it is to find recipes, track food usage, and make sustainable choices, building confidence in the app’s utility before download. Easy Navigation and Clear CTAs, With strategically placed calls-to-action, users are guided smoothly toward downloading the app, making the process seamless and reinforcing the app’s value.

Client's feedback

Mohamed Raouf

CEO Meals 4 less

We’re thrilled with how the website balances simplicity with impactful ideas that effectively communicate our goals. This design will be a strong foundation for connecting with users and encouraging them to join our food waste reduction journey.

Client

Merna Nazmy

The "Merna Nazmy Portfolio Website" project aimed to create a modern, user-friendly, and visually engaging online portfolio that effectively showcases Merna Nazmy’s work and skills. The objective was to design a website that would resonate with potential clients and employers, emphasizing Merna's unique style and professional strengths in a clean, easily navigable format.

The challenge

Merna's portfolio required a balance between aesthetics and functionality. The main challenges included: Highlighting her diverse skill set without overwhelming the viewer. Ensuring smooth navigation and quick access to her projects, particularly on mobile devices. Incorporating her personal brand identity in a way that stands out in a competitive field.

My solution

Developed custom visuals and a cohesive color scheme that aligned with Merna's brand, creating a memorable and professional impression. Created a structured, modular layout that categorized her projects clearly, allowing users to browse seamlessly. Added subtle animations and interactive elements to engage visitors while maintaining fast load times and smooth transitions between pages.

Mint Studio

Type

Web Design

Date

Sep 2024

Role

UI/UX designer

Client

Mint Studio

The "Mint Studio Website" project aimed to create an immersive online experience for a visual storytelling agency specializing in bringing brand narratives to life through compelling visuals. The goal was to design a website that captures the essence of Mint Studio's creative work, inviting potential clients to explore their portfolio and understand their approach to storytelling.

The challenge

The site needed to showcase Mint Studio's portfolio in a way that emphasized both quality and creativity, capturing attention without overwhelming the viewer. The design had to reflect Mint Studio’s distinct voice and storytelling approach, making their brand identity clear from the first interaction.

My solution

Created a full-screen gallery and portfolio layout to highlight Mint Studio's visual storytelling capabilities, using large images and video elements to engage users from the start. Integrated Mint Studio’s brand story through concise copy, custom illustrations, and strategic placement of call-to-action elements to lead users through their services and process. Designed an intuitive, minimalist navigation bar and page transitions to keep the focus on Mint Studio’s work while making it easy for users to explore.

Insight Agency

Web Design

Date

Sep 2024

Role

UIUX Designer

Client

Insight Agency

The "Insight Website" project involved creating a polished and engaging online presence for a digital marketing agency. Insight wanted a website that would effectively showcase their services, success stories, and marketing expertise to attract new clients and solidify their reputation in the digital marketing field. The primary goal was to design a site that highlighted their data-driven approach and innovative strategies while making it easy for potential clients to connect.

The challenge

As a digital marketing agency, Insight needed to project authority and credibility through every part of the website. The website needed clear pathways for visitors to understand services and take action, whether booking a consultation or contacting Insight directly.

My solution

Created a clean, professional design with strategic use of colors and typography to establish a trustworthy and authoritative brand presence. Developed a dedicated section for each service Insight offers, with concise descriptions and case studies that illustrate their success. This provided an easy-to-navigate structure that guides users through Insight’s offerings.
